Red Velvet Cack

  • Time: 55
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
table spoon red food color 1
cup plaon cack flour 1-2
tablespoon vennila esense 1
cup caster sugar 1-1/2
ange peel table spoon 1
teaspoon Butter for grees 8 egg 1
For iceing cup iceing sugar 1/2
cup creem cheese 1

Step 1: Preheat oven at 180°C. Line a 7-inch round cake tin by greasing with little butter and dusting with little refined flour.

Step 2: Beat eggs and castor sugar in a bowl till fluffy.

Step 3: Add vanilla essence and food colour and beat again.

Step 4: Add refined flour and fold lightly till well blended. Add orange rind and mix well.

Step 5: Pour batter in lined cake tin and put in the preheated oven. Bake for 30-40 minutes.

Step 6: To make icing, combine cream cheese and icing sugar in a bowl and whisk well till well blended.

Step 7: Remove cake from oven and demould. Discard the top rough layer and even it out. Slice diagonally from between. Spread icing evenly on the base. Cover with upper layer and spread icing evenly on this layer as well.

Step 8: Cut into wedges and serve.

Mathi Mulakitathu(Sardines in Spicy Curry)

  • Time: 40
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Mathi (Sardines,Cleaned and Washed) 1/2 kgs
Fenu Greek 1/2 tsp
Pearl Onions 15 nos
Garlic Cloves 6 nos
ginger 1 piece
Curry leave 1 sprig
Tomato(sliced) 1 nos
Salt some to taste 1 tsp
Tamarind(Lemon Sized diluted in water) 1 nos
Water 1 cups
Kashmiri Chilly Powder 1 tbsp
turmeric powder 1/4 tsp
green chillies 2-3 nos
Coconut Oil 3 tbsp

Step 1: First of all,mix tamarind,kashmiri chilly powder and turmeric powder in a cup of water and keep it aside.

Step 2: In a pan,heat oil and add fenugreek to it, and saute the mix by adding the pearl onions , garlic cloves,ginger.

Step 3: To this mix add the tomato and also the cleaned sardines(mathi) along with required salt.

Step 4: Add the spicy mix which prepared in the first step and boil it.

Step 5: When the gravy gets thickened,add the curry leaves and take out of flame.

Step 6: In a small vessel,crush green chillies,pearl onions in coconut oil.

Step 7: Mix this with curry well. And its ready to serve.

Step 8: Serves delicious with Rice.

Karimeen Fry (Pearlspot Fry)

  • Time: 70
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
KariMeen/ Pearl Spot 2 nos
turmeric powder 1 tsp
Red Chilli Powder 1 tsp
Kashmiri Chilly Powder 1/2 tsp
Ginger-garlic paste 2 tsp
Fennel powder 1/2 tsp
Salt 1 tsp
Curry leaves 1 spring
Oil – for frying 2 tbsp

Step 1: Clean the fish with cold water along with lime and salt, pat dry and place on a plate.

Step 2: Make deep slits on the body of the fish, on both sides.

Step 3: Take all the spice powders along with salt and add the ginger-garlic paste and form a thick paste, if required you can add few drops of lime juice.

Step 4: Apply on the fish and leave it for minimum 1 hour.

Step 5: Heat pan with oil and add curry leaves fry for a minute, then place the fish and let it cook on each side for 6-7 minutes such that the fish is fully cooked.

Step 6: Serve hot with rice and curry.
